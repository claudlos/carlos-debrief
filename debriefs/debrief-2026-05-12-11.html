⚡ Quick Summary

  • LLM safety bypassed by a single neuron: Researchers identified individual refusal neurons — each causally sufficient on their own — that can fully bypass safety alignment across seven models spanning 1.7B to 70B parameters, with no training or prompt engineering required.
  • Android VPN leak: A critical Android 16 bug disclosed by Mullvad allows any installed app to route traffic outside the VPN tunnel, exposing users who rely on VPN privacy.
  • Activation steering improved for multi-turn dialogue: GCAD fixes KV-cache contamination in residual-stream steering via attention-level gating, improving long-horizon coherence drift from −18.6 to −1.9.
  • Diffusion language models advance: ELF (Embedded Language Flows) diffuses in continuous embedding space and outperforms both discrete and prior continuous DLMs, suggesting a viable path to competitive continuous text generation.
  • Queryable LoRA for dynamic fine-tuning: A shared queryable memory of low-rank update atoms enables context-sensitive parameter-efficient adaptation without sacrificing the compactness of LoRA.

📰 News Headlines

  • Android VPN bypass vulnerability: Mullvad disclosed a sweeping Android 16 flaw where any app can leak VPN traffic, potentially affecting millions of privacy-conscious users.
  • LLM alignment fragility: The single-neuron bypass result shows that RLHF/alignment is far more mechanically fragile than the field assumed — a governance and deployment concern for frontier model providers.
  • AI-driven scientific kernel optimization: The Metal-Sci benchmark demonstrates frontier LLMs can evolve 10× faster compute kernels on Apple Silicon, with held-out tests catching silent correctness failures in-distribution scores miss.
  • Diffusion vs autoregressive text generation: Multiple concurrent papers (ELF, Dystruct) push DLM capabilities closer to AR models, signaling a new architecture competition in text generation.
  • Post-quantum cryptography for IoT: Growing literature addresses deploying post-quantum auth and encryption on resource-constrained IoT devices as quantum threat timelines sharpen.
