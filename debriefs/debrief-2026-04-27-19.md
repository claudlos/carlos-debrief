**[6] [EditCrafter: Tuning-free High-Resolution Image Editing via Pretrained Diffusion Model](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.10268)**
EditCrafter uses tiled inversion and noise-damped manifold-constrained guidance to enable high-quality image editing at arbitrary resolutions without fine-tuning, significantly exceeding the training resolution limits of standard diffusion-based editing methods.

**[7] [Abstain-R1: Calibrated Abstention and Post-Refusal Clarification via Verifiable RL](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.17073)**
Abstain-R1 is a 3B model trained with a clarification-aware RL reward that jointly optimizes abstention and post-refusal clarification on unanswerable queries, achieving behavior competitive with DeepSeek-R1 while learning via verifiable rewards rather than scale alone.


### Lobste.rs
**[1] [What's new in pip 26.1 - lockfiles and dependency cooldowns](https://ichard26.github.io/blog/2026/04/whats-new-in-pip-26.1/)**
pip 26.1 adds support for dependency cooldowns, experimental support for reading/installing from standard lockfiles (pylock.toml), fixes several long-standing limitations of the 2020 resolver, and drops support for Python 3.9.

**[2] [The woes of sanitizing SVGs](https://muffin.ink/blog/scratch-svg-sanitization/)**
Scratch has a long history of SVG-related vulnerabilities because it parses user-generated attacker-controlled content into the main document, enabling a broad attack surface through malicious SVG uploads.

**[3] [89 vulnerabilities in XAPI / Citrix XenServer](https://shittrix.moksha.dk/)**
Day-0 public disclosure of 89 independently exploitable vulnerabilities in Citrix XenServer, a major enterprise virtualization platform, highlighting systemic security risks in widely-deployed infrastructure software.

**[4] [ChatGPT Won't Let You Type Until Cloudflare Reads Your React State. I Decrypted the Program That Does It](https://www.buchodi.com/chatgpt-wont-let-you-type-until-cloudflare-reads-your-react-state-i-decrypted-the-program-that-does-it/)**
The author reverse-engineered Cloudflare's React state surveillance mechanism used in ChatGPT's input field, documenting how the CDN's JavaScript hooks into client-side React state before user input is permitted — a significant privacy finding.


### Google Scholar
**[8] [Catalyst breakthroughs in methane dry reforming: Employing machine learning for future advancements](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0360319924036942)**
Machine learning is applied to accelerate catalyst discovery and optimize reaction conditions for methane dry reforming — converting greenhouse gases CO2 and methane into useful synthesis gas — an environmentally important industrial chemistry application of ML.

**[9] [Advancements in Artificial Intelligence: Breakthroughs, Challenges and the Road Ahead](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/11050474/)**
A quantitative analysis of AI advancements from 2010 onwards reveals industry overtook academia in releasing notable AI models from 2014, with breakthrough spikes in 2013, 2016, and 2019 corresponding to major deep learning advances.

**[10] [Large language models (LLM) in computational social science: prospects, current state, and challenges](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s13278-025-01428-9)**
This review examines how LLMs are applied in computational social science for text analysis, sentiment detection, and social simulation, while addressing challenges around data bias, privacy, and model integration in social research contexts.

**[11] [Artificial intelligence and machine learning in cybersecurity: a deep dive into state-of-the-art techniques and future paradigms](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0360319924036942)**
A comprehensive review of AI/ML techniques applied to cybersecurity covering adversarial AI, automated threat intelligence, AI-driven security orchestration, and future paradigms for defending networks against AI-powered attacks.

**[12] [Securing the future: exploring post-quantum cryptography for authentication and user privacy in IoT devices](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10586-024-04799-4)**
With the emergence of quantum computers, traditional cryptographic methods are vulnerable to attacks; this paper reviews post-quantum cryptographic approaches for securing IoT devices and user privacy against future quantum threats.

**[13] [Quantum machine learning: A comprehensive review of integrating AI with quantum computing for computational advancements](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2215016125001645)**
This review covers the integration of AI with quantum computing, examining quantum-enhanced classical ML, native quantum algorithms, and hybrid approaches with applications in optimization, drug discovery, and quantum-secured communications.
