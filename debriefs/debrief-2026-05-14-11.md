- **Intrinsic Retrieval**: Researchers show attention-based encoder-decoder models can retrieve directly from their own internal representations, unifying retrieval and generation in a single system (INTRA) and outperforming engineered RAG pipelines.
- **Efficient Adaptation**: FAAST introduces forward-only associative learning that compiles labeled examples into fast weights analytically in a single pass, reducing adaptation time by 90%+ with constant inference cost.
