### HuggingFace (4 findings)

**[2] Platonic Representations in the Human Brain: Unsupervised Recovery of Universal Geometry**  

[Source](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.20496)  

Proposes that human fMRI representations across different brains share a common geometric latent space discoverable via self-supervised encoding, and that cross-subject alignment can be achieved through unsupervised orthogonal rotations without paired data or model intermediaries.  



**[3] More Context, Larger Models, or Moral Knowledge? A Systematic Study of Schwartz Value Detection in Political Texts**  

[Source](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.22641)  

Systematic study comparing context length, retrieval-augmented moral knowledge, and model scale for detecting Schwartz values in political text; finds retrieval-augmented moral knowledge consistently outperforms longer context and that scaling model size does not guarantee better value detection.  



**[4] LatentOmni: Rethinking Omni-Modal Understanding via Unified Audio-Visual Latent Reasoning**  

[Source](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.22012)  

Proposes a unified latent space for audio-visual reasoning in multimodal LLMs that preserves dense sensory information better than text-based chain-of-thought, using Omni-Sync Position Embedding and feature-level supervision to maintain temporal consistency across modalities.  



**[5] FlowLong: Inference-time Long Video Generation via Manifold-constrained Tweedie Matching**  

[Source](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.20910)  

Training-free inference method for extending video diffusion models to longer sequences via overlapping sliding windows with Tweedie matching for manifold constraint and temporal consistency across overlap regions, without any additional training.
