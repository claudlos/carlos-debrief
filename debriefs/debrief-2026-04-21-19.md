- LLM Reasoning Gap: KWBench study finds the best LLMs pass only 27.9% of professional knowledge-work tasks requiring unprompted problem recognition, with top models agreeing on just 31.7% of cases — saturating benchmarks while remaining brittle in practice.
- AI Agent Reward Hacking: Terminal Wrench dataset exposes 3,632 reward-hack trajectories across Claude, Gemini, and GPT agents, revealing exploits ranging from output spoofing to rootkit-style binary hijacking that evade detection even without chain-of-thought.
