### Lobste.rs

**TurboQuant: A First-Principles Walkthrough**
[https://arkaung.github.io/interactive-turboquant/](https://arkaung.github.io/interactive-turboquant/)
Modern language models store large tables of high-dimensional vectors: KV caches, embeddings, attention keys. TurboQuant compresses each coordinate of these vectors to 2–4 bits with provably near-optimal distortion, no memory overhead for scale factors, and no training or calibration. This page explains how it works.

**Email is crazy**
[https://samkhawase.com/blog/email-is-crazy/](https://samkhawase.com/blog/email-is-crazy/)
A deep dive into how email actually works — SMTP, MTAs, SPF/DKIM/DMARC authentication, spam filtering and TLS — traced step by step from Alice to Bob.

**ChatGPT Won't Let You Type Until Cloudflare Reads Your React State. I Decrypted the Program That Does It**
[https://www.buchodi.com/chatgpt-wont-let-you-type-until-cloudflare-reads-your-react-state-i-decrypted-the-program-that-does-it/](https://www.buchodi.com/chatgpt-wont-let-you-type-until-cloudflare-reads-your-react-state-i-decrypted-the-program-that-does-it/)

### HuggingFace Papers

**DiagramBank: A Large-scale Dataset of Diagram Design Exemplars with Paper Metadata for Retrieval-Augmented Generation**
[https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20857](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20857)
DiagramBank provides 89,422 schematic diagrams curated from top-tier scientific publications, paired with captions, abstracts, and figure-reference pairs for multimodal retrieval and exemplar-driven scientific figure generation — bridging the 'AI scientist' pipeline gap where publication-grade diagrams remain a bottleneck.

**EmbodiedMidtrain: Bridging the Gap between Vision-Language Models and Vision-Language-Action Models via Mid-training**
[https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20012](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20012)
EmbodiedMidtrain addresses the distribution gap between generic Vision-Language Models and robot action domains by mid-training VLMs on VLA-aligned data selected via a lightweight proximity estimator, achieving competitive results with expert VLAs at smaller scale and training budgets.

**Memanto: Typed Semantic Memory with Information-Theoretic Retrieval for Long-Horizon Agents**
[https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.22085](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.22085)
Memanto introduces a universal typed memory schema (13 categories), automated conflict resolution, and temporal versioning backed by a no-indexing semantic database achieving sub-90ms deterministic retrieval — reaching 89.8% on LongMemEval and 87.1% on LoCoMo, surpassing all hybrid graph and vector systems with a single query and zero ingestion cost.

**Video Analysis and Generation via a Semantic Progress Function**
[https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.22554](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.22554)
This work introduces a Semantic Progress Function — a 1D representation of how meaning evolves across video frames — enabling analysis, correction, and steering of semantic pacing in both generated and real-world video sequences by reparameterizing frames so semantic change unfolds at a constant rate.

**Building a Precise Video Language with Human-AI Oversight**
[https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.21718](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.21718)
This work introduces CHAI, a critique-based human-AI oversight framework for precise video captioning — trained experts revise model-generated pre-captions using structured visual primitives from professional filmmakers, and the resulting preference data fine-tunes Qwen3-VL via SFT and DPO to match or exceed Gemini-3.1-Pro.

**EditCrafter: Tuning-free High-Resolution Image Editing via Pretrained Diffusion Model**
[https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.10268](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.10268)
EditCrafter enables high-resolution (arbitrary aspect ratio) image editing without fine-tuning by combining tiled inversion that preserves identity with a noise-damped manifold-constrained classifier-free guidance (NDCFG++), extending pretrained T2I diffusion models beyond their training resolution limits.

**Abstain-R1: Calibrated Abstention and Post-Refusal Clarification via Verifiable RL**
[https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.17073](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.17073)
Abstain-R1 uses a clarification-aware RLVR reward to train a 3B model that abstains from unanswerable queries AND explains what information is missing, achieving unanswerable-query behavior competitive with DeepSeek-R1 — showing calibrated abstention and semantic clarification are learnable via verifiable rewards rather than emergent from scale.
