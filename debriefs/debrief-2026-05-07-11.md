## ⚡ Quick Summary
- <strong>Stream-R1 video generation:</strong> Proposes reliability-perplexity aware reward distillation that selectively emphasizes high-quality spatiotemporal regions and trustworthy rollouts, significantly improving autoregressive streaming video models.
- <strong>MiniCPM-o 4.5 full-duplex omni-modal:</strong> Achieves simultaneous perception and response in a 9B-parameter model via Omni-Flow temporal alignment, approaching Gemini 2.5 Flash vision benchmarks while enabling proactive behavior.
- <strong>Workspace-Bench 1.0:</strong> Introduces a 388-task benchmark with 20K+ real files and 20GB of workspace data; best AI agent scores 68.7% vs 80.7% human baseline, exposing significant gaps in cross-file dependency reasoning.
- <strong>When to Think, When to Speak:</strong> Introduces Side-by-Side interleaved reasoning that separates private deliberation from public disclosure, improving accuracy-latency Pareto trade-offs on Qwen3 models across AIME25 and GPQA-Diamond.
- <strong>The First Token Knows:</strong> Shows that single-decode first-token confidence (phi_first) matches multi-sample semantic self-consistency for hallucination detection at 0.820 AUROC, offering a low-cost uncertainty estimation baseline.
