## ⚡ Quick Summary

- <strong>Nemotron 3 Nano Omni</strong> (NVIDIA) introduces native audio multimodal support with novel token-reduction techniques, achieving 2–3× higher throughput than comparable open models across document understanding, video comprehension, and computer use tasks.
- <strong>Step-level cascade optimization</strong> for computer-use agents selectively routes only high-risk GUI interactions (stalls, semantic drift) to large multimodal models, dramatically reducing compute cost while maintaining accuracy.
- <strong>V-GRPO</strong> demonstrates that ELBO-based online RL can match traditional MDP-based approaches for diffusion model alignment, delivering 2–3× speedups over prior state-of-the-art methods.
- <strong>Praxy Voice</strong> achieves commercial-class Indic TTS (Telugu, Tamil, Hindi) using only a LoRA adapter and voice-prompt recovery — without any commercial TTS training data or acoustic decoder fine-tuning.
- <strong>FASH-iCNN</strong> reveals texture and luminance as the primary visual carriers of editorial fashion identity across 87K Vogue runway images, correctly identifying house at 78%, decade at 89%, and year at 58%.
