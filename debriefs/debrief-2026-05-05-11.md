### HuggingFace (2)

6. **Persistent Visual Memory: Sustaining Perception for Deep Generation in LVLMs** | HuggingFace | [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.00814)
   LVLMs suffer visual attention decay during long text generation due to expanded attention partition. This paper proposes Persistent Visual Memory (PVM), a lightweight module added alongside the FFN that provides direct distance-agnostic visual retrieval, structurally countering signal suppression in deep autoregressive generation. Validated on Qwen3-VL 4B/8B with consistent gains in complex reasoning tasks.
   *Why it matters:* Directly addresses a fundamental limitation of autoregressive LVLMs — visual forgetting — with a minimal overhead module that improves multimodal reasoning at scale.

7. **Talker-T2AV: Joint Talking Audio-Video Generation with Autoregressive Diffusion Modeling** | HuggingFace | [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.23586)
   Talker-T2AV separates high-level semantic cross-modal reasoning in a shared AR backbone from low-level modality-specific diffusion decoders, achieving better lip-sync, video quality, and cross-modal consistency than cascaded approaches.
   *Why it matters:* Demonstrates that modality entanglement at all levels is unnecessary for talking-head synthesis; architectural separation improves both quality and efficiency.
