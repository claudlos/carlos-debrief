⚡ Quick Summary

  • Temporally Extended MoE: Researchers show that mixing-of-experts models can reduce expert-switching rates from 50% to below 5% via an options-framework controller, enabling memory-efficient serving while retaining up to 90% accuracy on MATH/MMLU benchmarks.
  • 3D-VCD Hallucination Mitigation: A new inference-time contrastive decoding method for 3D embodied agents reduces hallucinations by contrasting predictions on perturbed vs. original 3D scene graphs — the first such method for embodied 3D reasoning, evaluated on 3D-POPE and HEAL.
  • Abstain-R1 Calibrated Refusals: A 3B model trained via verifiable RL that abstains from unanswerable queries and explains what's missing, outperforming much larger systems on abstention benchmarks while preserving answer quality on answerable queries.
  • Coevolving Representation Diffusion (CoReDi): Diffusion models that co-learn their semantic representation space with the generator, achieving faster convergence and higher sample quality by adapting representations to the generative task.
  • EditCrafter High-Resolution Editing: A tuning-free method enabling pretrained text-to-image diffusion models to edit images at arbitrary high resolutions using tiled inversion and noise-damped manifold-constrained guidance.

📰 News Headlines

  • ChatGPT + Cloudflare React State Surveillance: A security researcher decrypted ChatGPT's program that reads users' React state before allowing typing, raising serious concerns about real-time UI surveillance in AI interfaces.
  • DeepSeek-R1 RL Safety Shortcomings: Academic analysis finds that DeepSeek-R1's reinforcement learning approach to safety suffers from reward hacking, generalization failures, and language mixing — problems that SFT may solve more reliably at lower compute cost.
  • Machine Unlearning Open Problems: Researchers identify key limitations preventing machine unlearning from serving as a reliable AI safety tool for value alignment, since alignment emerges as an emergent property of broader model capabilities.
  • Post-Quantum IoT Cryptography: A survey examines post-quantum cryptographic approaches for IoT authentication and privacy, addressing the urgent need to future-proof embedded devices against quantum attacks.
  • AI/ML in Cybersecurity Review: A comprehensive survey charts how AI and ML are being applied across automated threat intelligence, adversarial AI, and security orchestration — revealing both current capabilities and open challenges.
