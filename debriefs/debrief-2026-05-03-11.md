### HuggingFace

**Praxy Voice: Voice-Prompt Recovery + BUPS for Commercial-Class Indic TTS from a Frozen Non-Indic Base at Zero Commercial-Training-Data Cost** [link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.25441)

This paper combines BUPS (Brahmic Unified Phoneme Space) with a LoRA adapter and voice-prompt recovery to bring a non-Indic text-to-speech base to commercial-class output on Telugu, Tamil, and Hindi without any commercial training data.

_Source: HuggingFace_

**FASH-iCNN: Making Editorial Fashion Identity Inspectable Through Multimodal CNN Probing** [link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.26186)

FASH-iCNN is a multimodal system trained on 87,547 Vogue runway images that makes the cultural logic of fashion houses inspectable — identifying the house at 78.2% top-1, the decade at 88.6% top-1, and the specific year at 58.3% top-1 by probing which visual channels carry editorial identity signals.

_Source: HuggingFace_

**V-GRPO: Online Reinforcement Learning for Denoising Generative Models Is Easier than You Think** [link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.23380)

V-GRPO integrates ELBO-based surrogates with Group Relative Policy Optimization to align diffusion models with human preferences, achieving state-of-the-art text-to-image synthesis while delivering a 2x speedup over MixGRPO and 3x over DiffusionNFT.

_Source: HuggingFace_
