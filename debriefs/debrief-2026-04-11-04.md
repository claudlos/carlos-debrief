# 🧪 AI Research Debrief — April 11, 2026 (43 papers)

---

## ⚡ Quick Summary

- 🛡️ **Your Agent Is Mine**: First systematic study of malicious LLM API routers with full plaintext access to tool-call payloads — reveals supply chain attack surface with no crypto integrity enforced.
- 🔬 **SUPERNOVA**: Extends RLVR beyond math/code to general reasoning (causal, temporal) via a new RL environment called GeneralThought — shows 8.3% avg improvement across 8 benchmarks.
- 🧠 **Seeing but Not Thinking**: Multimodal MoE models perceive images accurately but fail at reasoning over them while solving identical problems as text — routing distraction causes cross-modal reasoning gap.
- 📊 **PIArena**: Unified platform for prompt injection evaluation exposing that many defenses reported as effective exhibit brittle generalization across diverse attack settings.
- 🤖 **ClawBench**: 153 everyday online tasks across 144 live platforms reveal frontier AI agents still can't reliably automate routine life tasks like booking appointments or completing purchases.
