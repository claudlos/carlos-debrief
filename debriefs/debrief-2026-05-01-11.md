## ⚡ Quick Summary

> ArXiv research highlights this cycle:

- **LLMs & Post-Training**: Co-Evolving Policy Distillation unifies RLVR and OPD for all-in-one text/image/video reasoning, outperforming domain-specific experts; Operating-Layer Controls study reveals onchain LLM agent reliability comes from the operating layer (typed controls, validation) rather than the base model.
- **Vision & Generation**: ViPO scales visual preference optimization to 1M image pairs and 300K video pairs; PhyCo uses physics-supervised fine-tuning with VLM reward for physically consistent video; Representation Fréchet Loss enables FID-optimized one-step generators without distillation.
- **AI Agents**: Claw-Eval-Live benchmark shows leading models pass only 66.7% of real-world workflow tasks; Synthetic Computers at Scale generates 1,000 realistic computer environments for 8+ hour agent simulations; Eywa augments LLMs with domain-specific scientific models.
- **AI Safety**: Safety Drift study of 100 fine-tuned models reveals safety behavior is not stable under downstream adaptation — models improve on some instruments while degrading on others, raising governance questions for high-stakes deployments.
- **ML Efficiency**: RoundPipe enables LoRA fine-tuning of 235B-parameter models on a single 8×RTX 4090 server (2× speedup); Length Value Model predicts remaining generation horizon, improving GSM8K from 6% to 63% accuracy at a 200-token budget.

## 📰 News Headlines

> Top themes from Lobste.rs, Google Scholar, and Hacker News:

- **AI Memory Efficiency**: Google researchers announce chatbots can now use six times less memory during conversations without sacrificing performance, potentially dramatically reducing deployment costs.
- **AI Safety Under Fine-Tuning**: Safety Drift research exposes that fine-tuning routinely destabilizes safety behaviors in medical/legal AI models, raising governance concerns about base-model-only evaluation regimes.
- **AI Agents Still Struggle**: Live benchmarks like Claw-Eval-Live show workflow automation remains far from solved — leading models fail on HR, multi-system business workflows, and complex agent interactions.
- **AI Security Red-Teaming**: FlashRT framework dramatically reduces GPU requirements (2× speedup, 2-4× memory reduction) for evaluating prompt injection and knowledge corruption attacks on long-context LLMs.
- **Post-Quantum Cryptography**: Research highlights urgency of deploying post-quantum cryptography in IoT devices as quantum computing advances threaten traditional RSA/ECC encryption schemes.
