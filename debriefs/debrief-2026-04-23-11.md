### HuggingFace Papers
**COMPASS: COntinual Multilingual PEFT with Adaptive Semantic Sampling**  
COMPASS uses parameter-efficient fine-tuning with language-specific adapters and a distribution-aware sampling strategy to adapt LLMs to target languages while maximizing cross-lingual transfer and minimizing interference. Across Phi-4-Mini, Llama-3.1-8B, and Qwen2.5-7B, it consistently outperforms similarity-guided baselines on multilingual benchmarks.
Source: [HuggingFace Papers] | [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20720)

**Streaming Structured Inference with Flash-SemiCRF**  
Flash-SemiCRF replaces a prohibitively large edge-potential tensor with a compact prefix-sum lookup, enabling exact segment-level inference (semi-CRF) on previously intractable sequence lengths — including genomic-scale sequences exceeding 100,000 positions — via a fused Triton kernel with sublinear working memory.
Source: [HuggingFace Papers] | [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.18780)

**Benign Fine-Tuning Breaks Safety Alignment in Audio LLMs**  
The first systematic study of benign fine-tuning safety in Audio LLMs finds that benign audio data can elevate Jailbreak Success Rate from single digits to 87.12%, driven by acoustic proximity to harmful content in the model's embedding space. Two defenses — embedding-space filtering and a textual system prompt — reduce JSR to near-zero.
Source: [HuggingFace Papers] | [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.16659)

**OpenMobile: Building Open Mobile Agents with Task and Trajectory Synthesis**  
OpenMobile is an open-source framework for synthesizing mobile agent training data via a scalable task pipeline and learner-expert policy switching, producing fine-tuned Qwen2.5-VL and Qwen3-VL models that reach 64.7% on AndroidWorld — far surpassing existing open-data approaches while avoiding benchmark overfitting.
Source: [HuggingFace Papers] | [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.15093)

**Abstain-R1: Calibrated Abstention and Post-Refusal Clarification via Verifiable RL**  
Abstain-R1 uses a clarification-aware RLVR reward that jointly optimizes abstention and semantically aligned post-refusal clarification for unanswerable queries, achieving competitive unanswerable-query behavior with DeepSeek-R1 at just 3B parameters — suggesting calibrated uncertainty can be learned through verifiable rewards rather than scale alone.
Source: [HuggingFace Papers] | [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.17073)

**Image Generators are Generalist Vision Learners**  
Vision Banana demonstrates that image generation pretraining teaches models powerful visual representations rivaling domain-specialist models, achieving SOTA on segmentation (SAM3) and metric depth (Depth Anything) by reframing perception as RGB generation via lightweight instruction-tuning on Nano Banana Pro.
Source: [HuggingFace Papers] | [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20329)

**SmartPhotoCrafter: Unified Reasoning, Generation, and Optimization for Automatic Photographic Image Editing**  
SmartPhotoCrafter couples an Image Critic module with a Photographic Artist module in a reasoning-to-generation pipeline, using multi-stage training (foundation pretraining → reasoning-guided multi-edit supervision → coordinated RL) to perform automatic photo-realistic editing without explicit human instructions.
Source: [HuggingFace Papers] | [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.19587)
