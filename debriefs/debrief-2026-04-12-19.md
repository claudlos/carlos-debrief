### 🤗 HuggingFace Papers

**[3] The Master Key Hypothesis: Unlocking Cross-Model Capability Transfer via Linear Subspace Alignment**
*Published: 2026-04-07*

Proposes that model capabilities correspond to directions in a low-dimensional latent subspace transferable across models through linear alignment. Introduces UNLOCK, a training-free framework that extracts capability directions and transfers them between models of different scales, achieving up to 12.1% accuracy gains on MATH benchmarks.

🔗 [https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.06377](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.06377)

**[4] Faithful GRPO: Improving Visual Spatial Reasoning via Constrained Policy Optimization**
*Published: 2026-04-09*

Identifies that multimodal reasoning models trained with standard GRPO often produce Chain-of-Thought traces inconsistent with final answers and poorly grounded in visual evidence. Proposes Faithful GRPO (FGRPO), which enforces logical consistency and visual grounding as constraints via Lagrangian dual ascent, reducing inconsistency rates from 24.5% to 1.7%.

🔗 [https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.08476](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.08476)

**[5] ImplicitMemBench: Measuring Unconscious Behavioral Adaptation in LLMs**
*Published: 2026-04-09*

Introduces the first benchmark for evaluating implicit (non-declarative) memory in LLMs through procedural memory, priming, and classical conditioning. Tests 17 models and finds none exceed 66% accuracy, with dramatic asymmetries between inhibition (17.6%) and preference (75.0%) tasks, suggesting architectural innovations beyond parameter scaling are needed.

🔗 [https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.08064](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.08064)
