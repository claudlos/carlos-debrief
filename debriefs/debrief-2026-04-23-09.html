  • AI Security Evaluation: AVISE provides the first modular open-source framework for systematically identifying vulnerabilities and evaluating security across AI systems — filling a critical gap as AI deployments expand into critical infrastructure.
  • LLM Agents Cannot Reason Scientifically: A landmark 25,000+ agent-run study across 8 domains finds LLM-based scientific agents ignore evidence in 68% of traces and exhibit refutation-driven belief revision in only 26% — they execute workflows but lack epistemic self-correction.
  • SpeechParaling-Bench: New benchmark expands paralinguistic speech evaluation from under 50 to 100+ fine-grained features across English-Chinese parallel audio for Large Audio-Language Models.
