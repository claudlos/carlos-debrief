### HuggingFace

**minWM: A Full-Stack Open-Source Framework for Real-Time Interactive Video World Models** - https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.30263
minWM provides an end-to-end pipeline that converts existing bidirectional T2V/TI2V video foundation models into camera-controllable few-step autoregressive world models. It fine-tunes a bidirectional video diffusion model with camera control, then applies Causal Forcing / Causal Forcing++ to distill it into a few-step AR generator for low-latency rollout. Released on Wan2.1-T2V-1.3B and HY1.5-TI2V-8B.
Authors: Min Zhao, Hongzhou Zhu, Bokai Yan, Zihan Zhou, Yimin Chen | Upvotes: 49
