⚡ Quick Summary

  • SkillOpt achieves +23.5 accuracy points for GPT-5.5 in direct chat and +24.8 inside Codex/Claude Code agent loops — the first systematic controllable text-space skill optimizer, using scored rollouts and bounded add/delete/replace edits with validation-based acceptance.
  • DAR (Diffusion-Adaptive Routing) improves SiT-XL/2 by 2.11 FID on ImageNet 256x256 while requiring 8.75x fewer training iterations, establishing cross-layer information routing as an underexplored and orthogonal design axis in diffusion modeling.
  • StepAudio 2.5 unifies ASR, TTS, and Realtime spoken interaction in a single RLHF-aligned foundation model that matches or exceeds specialized systems across all three capabilities, advancing post-training paradigm from SFT to task-tailored RLHF.
  • Lens — a 3.8B T2I model — matches or surpasses 6B+ state-of-the-art models on benchmarks while using only 19.3% of typical training compute, enabled by 800M densely captioned image-text pairs with ~109-word GPT-4.1 captions and semantic VAE + strong language encoder.
  • LatentUMM introduces dual latent alignment for unified multimodal models, explicitly aligning in/out latent transformations at both modality and capacity levels to eliminate semantic drift under cross-modal transitions.

📰 News Headlines

  • VLM trustworthiness crisis: Researchers identify that state-of-the-art VLMs exhibit 'functional blindness' — bypassing visual bottlenecks via strong language priors — and propose the Semantic Sufficiency Criterion (SSC) as a new evaluation target beyond simplistic 'multimodal gain' metrics.
  • SciAtlas knowledge graph: A 43M-paper, 26-discipline academic knowledge graph with 157M entities and 3B triplets launches as a cognitive map for agentic deep research, replacing keyword/vector retrieval with neuro-symbolic topological reasoning.
  • Quantum-safe IoT: Post-quantum cryptography adoption accelerates as researchers publish new schemes for quantum-resistant authentication and multimedia encryption in IoT consumer electronics, ahead of anticipated Q-Day.
