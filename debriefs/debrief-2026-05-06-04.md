### 🤗 HuggingFace Papers (19)

1. **X2SAM: Any Segmentation in Images and Videos**  
   X2SAM is a unified multimodal LLM that extends any-segmentation capabilities from images to video, coupling an LLM with a Mask Memory module for temporally consistent video mask generation. It supports open-vocabulary, referring, reasoning, and interactive segmentation across both image and video inputs.  
   [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.00891)

2. **The TTS-STT Flywheel: Synthetic Entity-Dense Audio Closes the Indic ASR Gap Where Commercial and Open-Source Systems Fail**  
   A TTS-to-STT flywheel synthesizes ~22,000 entity-dense Indic-English code-mix utterances at under $50, enabling a LoRA fine-tune of Whisper that achieves 17x improvement over open-source SOTA and 3x over commercial systems on entity recognition for Telugu, with cross-language gains on Hindi and Tamil.  
   [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.03073)

3. **ESARBench: A Benchmark for Agentic UAV Embodied Search and Rescue**  
   ESARBench is the first comprehensive benchmark for MLLM-driven UAV agents in embodied Search and Rescue, using Unreal Engine 5 and AirSim with GIS-mapped photorealistic environments, 600 realistic tasks, dynamic weather and time-of-day variables.  
   [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.01371)

4. **StateSMix: Online Lossless Compression via Mamba State Space Models and Sparse N-gram Context Mixing**  
   StateSMix couples an online-trained Mamba SSM with nine sparse n-gram hash tables and arithmetic coding to achieve lossless compression requiring no pre-trained weights or GPU, beating xz-9e by 8.7% on enwik8-1MB while running in pure C with AVX2 SIMD.  
   [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.02904)

5. **TCDA: Thread-Constrained Discourse-Aware Modeling for Conversational Sentiment Quadruple Analysis**  
   TCDA combines Thread-Constrained DAG and Discourse-Aware RoPE (D-RoPE) to model multi-turn conversation structure for aspect-based sentiment extraction, filtering cross-thread noise while capturing utterance-level temporal progression, achieving state-of-the-art on two benchmark datasets.  
   [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.01717)

6. **Chain of Evidence: Pixel-Level Visual Attribution for Iterative Retrieval-Augmented Generation**  
   Chain of Evidence (CoE) is a vision-language RAG framework that feeds VLM models screenshots of retrieved documents to output precise bounding boxes around relevant evidence, eliminating text parsing bottlenecks and preserving spatial layout cues.  
   [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.01284)

7. **PatRe: A Full-Stage Office Action and Rebuttal Generation Benchmark for Patent Examination**  
   PatRe is the first benchmark modeling the full patent examination lifecycle (Office Action plus applicant rebuttal) across 480 real-world cases, reframing it as dynamic multi-turn justification-and-response and revealing critical differences between proprietary and open-source LLMs on legal reasoning tasks.  
   [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.03571)

8. **Reinforcement Learning for LLM-based Multi-Agent Systems through Orchestration Traces**  
   This paper frames RL for LLM multi-agent systems through orchestration traces, identifying three axes: eight reward families, eight credit-bearing units, and five sub-decisions (spawn, delegate, communicate, aggregate, stop), finding no prior explicit RL method for the stopping decision.  
   [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.02801)

9. **SplAttN: Bridging 2D and 3D with Gaussian Soft Splatting and Attention for Point Cloud Completion**  
   SplAttN replaces hard projection in multi-modal point cloud completion with Differentiable Gaussian Splatting, preventing Cross-Modal Entropy Collapse and enabling dense gradient flow between 2D images and 3D geometry, achieving SOTA on PCN and ShapeNet-55/34.  
   [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.01466)

10. **Beyond SFT-to-RL: Pre-alignment via Black-Box On-Policy Distillation for Multimodal RL**

11. **HeavySkill: Heavy Thinking as the Inner Skill in Agentic Harness**

    [Link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.02396)

12. **Healthcare AI GYM for Medical Agents**

13. **SVGS: Enhancing Gaussian Splatting Using Primitives with Spatially Varying Colors**
