### HuggingFace Papers

**1. [Praxy Voice: Voice-Prompt Recovery + BUPS for Commercial-Class Indic TTS](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.25441)**
   - Authors: Venkata Pushpak Teja Menta
   - Presents BUPS (Brahmic Unified Phoneme Space) and a LoRA adapter for the Chatterbox TTS base that enables commercial-class Indic speech synthesis (Telugu, Tamil, Hindi) without any commercial training data — achieving parity with proprietary systems.

**2. [FASH-iCNN: Making Editorial Fashion Identity Inspectable Through Multimodal CNN Probing](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.26186)**
   - Authors: Morayo Danielle Adeyemi, Ryan A. Rossi, Franck Dernoncourt
   - A multimodal probing system trained on 87,547 Vogue runway images that can identify which fashion house, era, and colour tradition shaped a garment — revealing that texture and luminance, not colour, carry the strongest editorial identity signal.

**3. [V-GRPO: Online Reinforcement Learning for Denoising Generative Models](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.23380)**
   - Authors: Bingda Tang, Yuhui Zhang, Xiaohan Wang, Jiayuan Mao, Ludwig Schmidt
   - Introduces Variational GRPO (V-GRPO), which stabilises and accelerates ELBO-based policy-gradient RL for diffusion models — achieving state-of-the-art text-to-image quality with 2–3× speedup over prior MDP-based methods.
